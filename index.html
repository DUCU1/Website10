Overall aim of the game


Take 5 is a fast-paced card game that is perfect for larger groups of people who want to play a simple and fun game. The aim of the game is to avoid taking cards with bulls and to score as few bulls as possible. Each round, players simultaneously choose a card from their hand and place it face down on the table. When everyone has chosen a card, they are revealed and placed in ascending order on the table. If a player has played the sixth card in a row on the table, they must take all of those cards and add them to their bull pile. The game ends when someone has collected five bulls.
