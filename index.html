Welcome To Take5


In this version of the game, the overall aim is to be the player with the most points at the end of each round.
To achieve this, players must strategically choose which cards to play each round, while also trying to avoid taking penalty points AKA bulls. The game involves a combination of luck and skill, with players needing to think carefully about their moves in order to come out on top.
